I Love FC Barcelona

Background: FC Barcelona was founded more than a century ago by a diverse group of European men. It consisted of Catalan, Swiss, German, and English players. It's founder, Joan Gamper, was an avid athlete who participated in multiple sports. He became a resident of Barcelona and had decided to create a football club. Little did he know, that football club of his would produce the some of the best athletes the world has ever seen, and change football history forever.
